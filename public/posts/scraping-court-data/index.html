R for justice transparency

December 7, 2019 One minute read

I’m Ryan Gentzler, Director of Open Justice Oklahoma at the Oklahoma Policy Institute.

Over the past three years, I’ve developed systems almost exclusively in R to scrape, analyze, visualize, and communicate data related to Oklahoma’s justice system. As a state with one of the highest incarceration rates in the country, the need for a more transparent and accountable justice system could not be greater. Oklahoma also happens to have one of the most easily accessible and comprehensive court websites in the country, providing a unique opportunity to measure our justice system in real time.
